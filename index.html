Vigan City

Vigan, is renowned for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture and rich cultural heritage. Located in Ilocos Sur, this historic city offers a glimpse into the past with its cobblestone streets, ancestral houses, and iconic Calle Crisologo. A unique blend of Filipino, Chinese, and Spanish influences is reflected in its architecture, traditions, and cuisine, making Vigan a must-visit destination for history and culture enthusiasts.
